## When do you need a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if you want to:

- Embed code-crew in a proprietary (closed-source) product
- Offer code-crew (or a derivative) as a hosted / managed service without releasing
  your modifications under AGPL-3.0
- Use code-crew in an enterprise environment under terms incompatible with AGPL-3.0

## FAQ

**Can I use code-crew internally at my company for free?**  
Yes, as long as you comply with AGPL-3.0 (source must be available to users of any
network service you run). Most internal tooling qualifies.

**Can I fork and sell a modified version?**  
Under AGPL-3.0: only if you also release your modifications. Under a commercial
license: yes.

**Do I need a CLA to contribute?**  
Yes — see [CLA.md](CLA.md). This allows the project to offer dual licensing while
keeping the open-source version genuinely free.
